COOL STUFF

I want to dedicate this section to cool things I have found through classes and mindless web searching. However, it may turn out to just become an area that I use to look impressingly deep and sophisticated.

The Martian  by Andy Weir was recommended to me by a dear friend. It is an enthralling tale of a stranded astronaut on the surface of Mars, and his consequent struggles to stay alive. Although I am of the opinion that we should colonize the Moon before taking part in any sort of Mars shenanigans, the book appealed to my deep passion for exploring space and planets and NASA in general. This book reminded me of the endless joy that is reading so definitely read it to find out what happens!

"The Last Question" by Isaac Asimov is a piece that I stumbled upon when I was pondering the extent to which technology would infiltrate our lives in the future. Rather than stimulate new thoughts and introspection, the idea posited by this short story planted itself into my brain and now I take its conclusion as the inevitable truth mankind is destined for. Great read!

Unsplash has some awesome pictures (for free!) that are awesome for desktop backgrounds, casual time-wasting, and making you look artsy in front of your friends. Definitely check it out, especially if you are making a personal website that needs a picture of pebbles on a beach to make it look good!
